We have a PPA!

We you, and we have setup a PPA for you to easily install of various Numix artworks in Ubuntu. To add the Numix PPA in your Ubuntu, just open a Terminal and run the following,

sudo add-apt-repository ppa:numix/ppa

Icon design

We use white pictograms on vividly coloured backgrounds for applications icons. The background colour is determined by the branding colour of the application. For action and status icons, we use pure monochrome and symbolic icons.

Elements

The theme elements follow a mostly flat design, sometimes with subtle shadow effects and a "2px" border radius. We use Open Sans as our standard font.

About Numix

Numix is a collection of modern and beautiful artworks. It follows a flat design approach, while still retaining some depth.
