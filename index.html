• Numix Project

Not just yet another theme suite

Numix is all about making a difference in theming. We aim to prove that "difference" and "usability" are two words combined, that can make sense. What you will get with Numix, is a modern looking, flat and stylish desktop, spiced up with a pinch of warmth just enough to make you feel like lying in your favourite comfy armchair, with a beverage of your choice in hand.

So, if you wanna have a taste of a cuppa something different or just long for theming your desktop in a jazzy way, Numix might just be the thing you need. :)

We have a PPA!

We you, and for your convenience, we have made a PPA to easily install various Numix artworks in Ubuntu. To add it to your Ubuntu, fire up a Terminal and run the following:

sudo add-apt-repository ppa:numix/ppa

Icon design

We use white pictograms on vividly coloured backgrounds for applications icons. The background colour is determined by the branding colour of the application. For action and status icons, we use pure monochrome and symbolic icons.

Elements

The theme elements follow a mostly flat design, sometimes with subtle shadow effects and a "2px" border radius. We use Open Sans as our standard font.

About Numix

Numix is a collection of modern and beautiful artworks. It follows a flat design approach, while still retaining some depth.
